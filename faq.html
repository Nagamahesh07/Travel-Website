Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our travel packages, bookings, payments, and services.

1. How do I book a trip with MITA?

You can book your trip through our Book Your Trip page by filling out the booking form and selecting your preferred destination.

2. Are flights included in the tour packages?

Some packages include flights, while others do not. The package details will clearly mention what is included.

3. Can I cancel or change my booking?

Yes. You can request a cancellation or change, subject to our booking and cancellation policies.

4. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, debit cards, credit cards, net banking, and other secure online payment methods.

5. Do you offer customized tour packages?

Yes! We can create personalized travel packages based on your budget, destination, and travel preferences.

6. Is travel insurance included?

Travel insurance is optional and may be included in selected premium packages.

7. What documents are required for international travel?

You'll generally need a valid passport and, depending on the destination, a visa.

8. How can I contact MITA for support?

You can reach us by phone, email, or by visiting our office during working hours.

9. Are group discounts available?

Yes. We offer special discounts for family trips, student groups, and corporate bookings.

10. What should I pack for my trip?

Pack comfortable clothing, essential documents, medications, chargers, and destination-specific items.
